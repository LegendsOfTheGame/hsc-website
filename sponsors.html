Community Partners

Power the Mission

Hammer Street Clean runs on commitment — and on the support of businesses that believe in what a clean neighbourhood does for everyone who lives and works in it.

// Corporate Partnership Tiers

Choose Your Level of Partnership

We're not asking for charity — we're inviting your business to be part of something visible. Every tier directly funds the work: the gear, the hours, and the presence on the street. Pick the level that fits, and let's build a cleaner #HamOnt together.

🏪

The Corner Store

CA$50/month

For the small shop that wants to ensure their slice of the street stays clean.

  • Name listed on the HSC Partners page
  • Social media shoutout on joining
  • Funds gloves, bags, and daily litter patrol supplies
  • Focus on sidewalk maintenance and immediate-area cleanup
Become a Partner — $50/mo

Annual: $450

The Toolbox

CA$125/month

This funds the actual gear — solvents, heavy-duty bags, and the tools that make the work possible.

  • Everything in The Corner Store
  • Name + logo listed on the Partners page
  • Quarterly cleanup report credited to your sponsorship
  • Featured in relevant social media posts and cleanup videos
Become a Partner — $125/mo

Annual: $1,310

The Smoke Signal

CA$250/month

A smoke signal is seen from a distance — a beacon. This business isn't just helping, they're leading the charge.

  • Everything in The Toolbox
  • Logo featured prominently on the Partners page
  • Named partnership at major events (e.g. Sew Hungry festival)
  • Direct point of contact with the HSC team
  • City-wide advocacy and community representation
Become a Partner — $250/mo

Annual: $2,435
