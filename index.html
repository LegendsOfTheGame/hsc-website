// City of Hamilton Grant Program

The City May Pay Half Your Graffiti Removal

Through the City of Hamilton's Commercial District Revitalization Grant, eligible commercial and mixed-use properties can get back up to 50% of the invoice — max $200 per incident, up to 5 incidents per year ($1,000/yr total). We take the photos, help with the paperwork, and do the removal. You could pay half of what you expected.

// 03 — About

Your Neighbours,
Not a Franchise.

Hammer Street Clean is a small, grassroots operation based in Hamilton, Ontario. We hold the street maintenance contract for Barton Village BIA, sit on Ottawa Street BIA's Streetscape & Beautification Committee, and run a daily weather report Hamiltonians actually check — we even produce the daily weather for Brandon, Manitoba.

A clean storefront says: someone is home here, someone cares. That's the whole business.

  • Bylaw-smart. We know Hamilton's property standards bylaw (10-118) — graffiti-free exteriors, waste-free frontage — and keep you on the right side of it.
  • Grant-fluent. We've walked owners through the City's graffiti grant paperwork before. It's genuinely not hard when someone shows you.
  • Everything documented. GPS-logged visits, timestamped photos, written reports. Your money buys receipts, not promises.
