// Eligibility

Who Qualifies

The grant is aimed at commercial and mixed-use properties across Hamilton — exactly the storefronts and street-facing walls we work on every day.

  • Commercial and mixed-use properties in the City of Hamilton.
  • Graffiti removal specifically — the incident needs to be graffiti, not general property maintenance.
  • Up to 5 incidents per property per year, each capped at $200 covered (50% of invoice), for a maximum of $1,000/year.

Not sure if your property qualifies? Ask us — we'll tell you straight, and if it doesn't qualify we'll still quote you a fair price for the removal itself.

// Worth Knowing

Waiting Costs More Than the Grant Saves

Hamilton's property standards bylaw (10-118) requires graffiti-free exteriors. Once a report leads to a bylaw notice, you have 13 days to clean it — or the City sends its own crew and bills you, even if you clean it late. A quick call to us is cheaper than waiting.

// Questions

Fair Questions, Straight Answers

Does the grant cover my monthly Storefront Care Plan?

No — the grant is specifically for graffiti removal incidents, not routine maintenance visits. But if graffiti hits between your plan visits, the same grant applies to that removal, and plan customers get our grant paperwork help included at no extra cost.

Do I have to apply for the grant myself?

No. We take the before-and-after photos and help you complete and submit the paperwork — you don't have to figure out a City form on your own.

What if my property isn't eligible?

We'll tell you honestly before any work starts. If it turns out you don't qualify, we'll still quote you a fair, straightforward price for the removal itself.

How much will I actually pay?

Up to 50% of the invoice, capped at $200 per incident. On a typical single-incident job, that often means you pay roughly half of what you'd expect — the exact amount depends on your invoice total and eligibility.
