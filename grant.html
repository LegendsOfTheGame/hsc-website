City of Hamilton Program

Graffiti Removal Grant:
Help for Hamilton Businesses

Did you know Hamilton offers a permanent grant program to help commercial and mixed-use property owners clean up graffiti — at no cost to you? Hammer Street Clean can help you access it.

Get Help Applying
$200 Per incident of graffiti removal
$1,000 Maximum per property per year

About the Program

The City of Hamilton's Commercial District Revitalization Grant Program permanently offers grants to cover graffiti removal costs for eligible properties. If you're a business owner in Barton Village, Downtown Hamilton, or any of the city's designated commercial corridors, you may qualify for financial support.

By removing graffiti quickly, property owners help deter future vandalism and significantly improve the aesthetic and safety of their surroundings — and the City wants to help make that happen.

Key Details

  • ◆ The Commercial District Revitalization Grant Program is a permanent City of Hamilton initiative.
  • ◆ Owners or tenants in commercial or mixed-use buildings can apply — you don't have to own the property.
  • ◆ Covers up to $200 per incident, for a maximum of 5 incidents per property ($1,000 per calendar year).
  • ◆ Properties must be located in designated corridors or BIAs. Check your eligibility on the City's map →
  • ◆ No application fee is required for the graffiti removal portion of the grant.

What You Need to Apply

  1. 01
    Document the graffiti

    Take a clear digital photo of the graffiti, including the date and location. (We can do this for you.)

  2. 02
    Prove occupancy or ownership

    Provide documentation showing you own or rent the property — a lease agreement or municipal tax bill works.

  3. 03
    Fill out the application form

    Complete the official City of Hamilton application form →

  4. 04
    Get the graffiti removed

    Schedule removal with Hammer Street Clean. We work quickly and professionally.

  5. 05
    Submit after photos

    Take a photo of the cleaned area (dated and location-identified) and submit it to the City to complete your claim.

How Hammer Street Clean Supports You

We don't just remove the graffiti — we support you through the entire grant process:

  • ◆ We take before and after photos that meet the City's documentation requirements.
  • ◆ We help you organize paperwork and understand the application process.
  • ◆ If you're unsure about eligibility, we'll walk you through it — no obligation.
  • ◆ We perform the actual removal using specialized agents, safely and thoroughly.
