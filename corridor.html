// Community Corridor Maintenance

Your Whole Block.
Funded Together.

Inside a BIA, members collectively fund street-level care — and it shows. Outside one, your block is on its own. Our shared sponsorship model fixes that: neighbouring businesses and organizations split the cost of professional weekly maintenance for the whole block, the same way BIA members fund theirs.

Two committed sponsors per block is all it takes — then weekly service starts, often within the week.

// What's Included

The Same Care the BIA Gets

  • Weekly corridor sweep — sidewalks, gutters, and storefronts along your frontage.
  • Entrance clearance — debris, litter, and seasonal buildup removed at access points.
  • Graffiti & damage reporting — documented and reported to the City on your behalf, with photos and GPS.
  • Direct contact — one person, one number, no call centre.
