// Corporate Sponsorship

Businesses Back the Block at 5×

Corporate sponsorship runs at five times the individual rate of whichever tier you choose — available in monthly, six-month, or annual terms. Example: a corporate Street Champion is $75/month, with your business name in the credits and reports instead of a personal one.

Corporate backers also get their business name listed on this website, with a link — and priority over same-day individual supporters whenever shout-outs, cleanup reports, and credits get scheduled.

// Where It Goes

Your Money Buys
Visible Work

Membership isn't charity into a void. It funds supplies for community cleanups the contracts don't cover, keeps the free daily weather service running, and pays for graffiti removal on blocks where nobody's invoicing anyone. Every cleanup is GPS-logged and photographed — members literally see the receipts.

  • Block Cleaner and up: your name goes on a real cleanup report, posted publicly.
  • Street Champion and up: you're in the video credits our followers actually watch.
  • Founding Ally: ten people, maximum, ever. When they're gone, they're gone.
