// Weather Report Sponsorship

Your Logo.
Your Day.
Every Channel.

Every single day — 365 days a year — Hammer Street Clean publishes a branded Hamilton weather card to Instagram, Facebook, X, and Bluesky. Local businesses sponsor it one day of the week at a time, for five bucks a month. Monday, Friday, and Saturday are already spoken for.

// Why It Works

Weather Is the One Post
Nobody Skips

People don't follow ads. They follow useful things — and nothing is more reliably useful than "what's it like outside today in Hamilton." That's why our daily cards and severe weather alerts get looked at, saved, and shared.

  • Daily, automated, never missed. The post goes out every morning on infrastructure that runs itself — your logo isn't waiting on anyone remembering to post.
  • 4 channels per post. Instagram, Facebook, X, and Bluesky — one sponsorship covers all of them, every week on your day.
  • An audience that keeps growing. Our real-time severe weather alerts — heat warnings, storm warnings, posted within hours of Environment Canada issuing them — are what earn the follows that see your logo.
  • Hyper-local audience. Followers are Hamilton residents and business owners — exactly the people who can walk into your shop.
  • Community halo. You're not buying an ad; you're sponsoring a free community weather service run by the street cleaning crew. That's a good look.
